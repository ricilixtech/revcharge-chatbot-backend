⚡ CHARGER TYPES & FEATURES
22. What is the difference between tethered and untethered chargers?

Tethered: Cable permanently attached.

Untethered: Detachable cable for a cleaner appearance.

23. What is the difference between smart and basic chargers?

Smart chargers offer app control, scheduling, energy monitoring, solar integration, and load balancing.
Basic chargers provide straightforward plug-in charging without app features.

24. What is single-phase charging?

Single-phase charging is standard in most Australian homes and adds approximately 30–40 km of range per hour.

25. What is three-phase charging?

Three-phase charging is faster and can add up to 120 km of range per hour. It requires a three-phase property connection.

26. What is a Level 2 charger?

Level 2 chargers are 5–7 times faster than a standard household power outlet and typically charge a vehicle fully in 4–8 hours.

☀️ SOLAR & ENERGY INTEGRATION
27. Can EV chargers work with solar panels?

Yes. Revcharge offers dedicated solar integration options.

28. Can chargers integrate with batteries and grid power?

Yes. Chargers can integrate with solar panels, home batteries, and grid power.

29. What is bidirectional charging?

Bidirectional chargers allow:

V2H (Vehicle-to-Home): Power your home from your car

V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid): Sell power back to the grid

30. How does Revcharge check charger compatibility with solar inverters?

Revcharge checks both products in inventory, runs a compatibility comparison, and provides results to the customer.

🔌 CHARGING & USAGE
31. How fast is home charging compared to a standard power outlet?

A standard outlet (~2.4kW) may take 24+ hours.
A Level 2 single-phase charger (~7kW) takes around 4–8 hours.
A Level 2 three-phase charger (~22kW) takes around 1–2 hours.

32. How much does it cost to charge an EV at home?

Typically $10–$20 per full charge, depending on electricity rates.

33. How often do EV owners charge?

Most EV owners charge 2–4 times per week. Daily commuters often charge overnight.

34. Is overnight charging safe?

Yes. Chargers include temperature monitoring and automatic shutoff for safety.

35. What happens during a power outage?

Charging stops safely and automatically resumes when power returns.

36. Will charging overload my home?

Smart chargers use load management to balance electricity usage and prevent overload.
